Cloud Copy

About

Cloud Copy is a modern, novel solution to clipboard management. It allows you to copy something on one device, and paste it directly into another, seamlessly. No hassle. No middle man. Your universal clipboard - Cloud Copy.

Cloud Copy differentiates itself from other clipboard applications by not depending on Chrome, or any browser you use. Versatile accross all platforms, connect all your devices with a single account.
